The Artisan Behind the Art

For over 15 years, Amatullah Chitalwala has been the heart and soul behind every exquisite rida that bears her name. What began as a passion project in her Pune home has blossomed into a cherished destination for Bohra women worldwide.

Amatullah's journey is woven with threads of tradition, innovation, and unwavering dedication to preserving the cultural heritage of the Dawoodi Bohra community while infusing contemporary elegance.
